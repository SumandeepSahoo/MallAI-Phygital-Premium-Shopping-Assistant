## 📌 Problem Statement

Premium malls today face a major shift in customer expectations:

* 🛍️ Declining footfall in physical stores
* 📦 Idle inventory in stockrooms
* ⚡ No presence in 10–20 min / 1–2 hr delivery market
* 🎯 Difficulty balancing **luxury experience + fast fulfillment**

👉 Over **60% of urban users expect same-day delivery**, but premium retail is lagging behind.

---

## 💡 Solution: MallAI

MallAI is an **AI-powered phygital platform** that bridges the gap between offline retail and online convenience.

### 🔹 Dual Mode Experience

**1. Physical Mode 🏬**

* Indoor navigation to exact product location (floor, aisle, rack)
* Reduced search time → improved in-store experience

**2. Online Mode 📱**

* Virtual store browsing (catalog + assisted shopping)
* AI recommendations
* 🚚 1–2 hour delivery from store

## ⚙️ Simulation Model

We built a **Python-based simulation** to validate operational feasibility.

### 🔬 What it does:

* Generates 100 random orders
* Groups orders into **30-minute batches**
* Simulates picking + delivery times
* Calculates SLA compliance

📂 Location: `simulation/simulation_model.py`

### 📊 Output:

* Delivery time distribution
* SLA success rate
* Average delivery metrics

## 🏭 SOP & Operations

MallAI ensures smooth operations using structured SOPs:

### 🧍 Physical Mode

* Assisted shopping
* Guided navigation
* Seamless checkout

### 📦 Online Mode

* Dedicated picking staff
* Separate packing zone
* Scheduled batch processing (every 30 mins)

### 👥 Roles

* Sales Associates
* Fulfillment Specialists
* Store Manager
